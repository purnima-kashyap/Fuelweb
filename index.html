What Are The Signs A Commercial Roof Needs Replacement?

If your commercial roof is leaking, it's a clear sign that it needs to be replaced. However, there are other indications that someone with expertise would be able to spot. For instance, blisters or cracks may appear on the roof, indicating that it needs to be replaced. Similarly, if the flashing is damaged or the seals are failing, it's a sign that the roof is nearing the end of its life.

How Long Does A Commercial Roof Replacement Usually Take?

The duration of a commercial roof replacement can vary depending on several factors, including the size of the roof, the complexity of the project, and the weather conditions. In general, a commercial roof replacement can take anywhere from a few days to a few weeks to complete. It's important to consult with a professional roofing contractor who can assess the specific requirements of your commercial roof replacement project and provide you with a more accurate estimate of the time it will take to complete.

What Is The Lifespan Of A Commercial Roofing System?

The lifespan of a commercial roofing system can range from 20 to 50 years, depending on factors such as the type of roofing system employed and the local weather conditions. Regular inspections and ongoing maintenance completed by trained professionals can significantly extend the longevity of commercial roofing systems.
